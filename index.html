Currently based in New Orleans, but like to venture outdoors. I have the most amazing memories diving in the deep sea to backpacking in Nepal. I can't wait for my next adventure with my travel partner, my sister.

Triathlon

All my extra time and energy is spent on the bike trainer or running outside. It all began when my sister had asked to go to Hawaii as a celebration to completing Nursing school... and doing 70.3 Hawaii. It was an amazing experience working through a routine and getting fitter during COVID. I was also fortunate enough to qualify to the 70.3 World Championships in St. George, Utah! That was an unforgettable experience with a battle against waves in the reservoir swim to hail on the bike. Thankfully, the sun was out and absolutely beautiful for the run. Right now, I am training for 70.3 Ironman Chattanooga on May 22, 2022.
